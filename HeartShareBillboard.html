HeartShare Billboard at MCU Park

HeartShare partnered with the Cyclones at MCU Park in Brooklyn, NY, to come up with a 32ft x 8ft billboard to be displayed the entire 2018 season to raise money for the non-profit. I drafted and executed the design using catchy colors and a font similar to varsity sports to entice the batter to hit the ball toward the billboard.
